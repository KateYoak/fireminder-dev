# Getting Started

Welcome to Fireminder! Here's how to begin.

## Create Your First Deck

1. Click **New Card** in the top right
2. Add content you want to remember
3. Your card will appear for review based on the deck's interval

## Review Cards

When cards are due:

1. Read the content
2. Optionally add a **reflection** (great for reinforcing memory!)
3. Choose your interval: shorter, default, or longer
4. Click **Review Done**

## Customize Your Experience

- **Target Cards**: Limit how many cards show per day
- **Max New Cards**: Control how many new cards enter rotation daily
- **Starting Interval**: Set how long until first review
