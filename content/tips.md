# Tips & Tricks

## Write Better Cards

- **One idea per card** - simpler is better
- **Use your own words** - paraphrasing improves memory
- **Add context** - use reminders for "why this matters"

## Review Effectively

- **Add reflections** - connecting ideas deepens understanding
- **Be honest** with intervals - if unsure, choose shorter
- **Review daily** - consistency beats cramming

## Organize Your Decks

- **Separate by topic** - different subjects, different decks
- **Use interval units wisely** - hours for language, weeks for concepts
- **Schedule cards** for future review when you'll need them

## Power Features

- **Calendar view** - see your review history at a glance
- **Import/Export** - back up or share your decks
- **Time travel** (developer) - test future scenarios
